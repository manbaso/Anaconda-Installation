# Anaconda-Installation

#### Step 1: Go to the link below to download Anaconda 

    https://www.anaconda.com/
    
![anaconda 0](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/46a9207f-9ea6-4ef7-9d99-41993b007ec5)

#### Step 2: Click on "Free Download" at the top right corner as shown in the screenshot

![anaconda 00](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ec7723fd-a320-4d19-af9d-50add6772799)

#### Step 3: Click on "Skip Registration" to proceed to download the application artifact

![anaconda 000](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/deeac725-93f6-4ad0-bd27-f914b6b53f15)

#### Step 4: Click on "64-Bit Graphical Installer (912.3M)" to download the Windows installer or click on "64-Bit (Apple silicon) Graphical Installer (704.7M)"
#### or click on "64-Bit (Apple silicon) Graphical Installer (704.7M)" to download the Mac installer as the case may be

![anaconda 1](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c198c8b5-a6f6-45fe-bfb1-3b05d1917131)

#### Step 5: Locate the installer that was just downloaded and double-click on it

#### Step 6: Click on "Next" to begin the installation

![anaconda 2](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d43cdbfc-3530-4e00-b889-f2867640d8f4)

#### Step 7: Click on "I Agree" to accept the licensing terms of Anaconda software 

![anaconda 3](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ee737a9f-9089-47ce-aece-8b9e83c5bb9c)

#### Step 8: Accept "Just Me(Recommended)" and click on "Next"

![anaconda 4](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/da3f21d5-70fb-4af5-b606-ce0b247bcd03)

#### Step 9: The installer will choose the default location for the installation. 
#### Click on "Next" to continue

![anaconda 5](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ac033dd4-615a-4ee1-95d7-0feaf913612b)

#### Step 10: You may check the box beside "Register Anaconda3 as my default python3.12. 
#### Though recommended, checking the box is optional. Click on "Next" to continue

![anaconda 6](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dc98a6df-e3a9-451b-8d39-f64e33fbe79b)

#### Step 11: Click on "Next" to continue the installation

![anaconda 7](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c6e097fa-e8a7-4ce5-a161-6cb23109c2fc)

#### Step 12: Click on "Finish" to complete the installation

![anaconda 8](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/312d0e44-557f-48cd-b863-c7ef225f9ebd)

#### Step 13: Go to the Windows search box and locate or search for "Anaconda Navigator". 
#### Open the navigator

#### Step 14: Updating Anaconda is optional. Make your choice and enjoy

![anaconda 10](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b153076e-af4c-446a-a3ef-89884529d6f5)
